# TuniBless Tozeur

Oasis of Volunteering — Tozeur. A non-profit association founded in 2021 that provides free guidance for Tunisian youth aiming to study or work in Germany. This repository hosts the static website for the Tozeur branch.

## Highlights

- 29K+ youth accompanied across Tunisia
- 24 regional branches
- 100% free and non-profit
- Founded in 2021

## Website Overview

The site is multilingual (Arabic, English, German) and includes:

- **About** the Tozeur branch and its mission
- **Volunteer activities** preparing youth for Germany
- **Organization structure** (national, regional, and local coordinators)
- **International partnerships** call-to-action
- **Contact** details and location

## Volunteer Activities in Tozeur

- Free German language classes
- Migration file preparation workshops
- Environmental initiatives in the oasis
- Youth leadership training programs

## Organization Structure

- National coordinator (Tunisia)
- Regional coordinators across 24 regions
- Tozeur branch coordinator
- Local coordinators in Tozeur, Degache, and Nefta

## Partnerships

We are seeking partners in Germany. Partnerships focus on:

- Field collaboration and joint activities in Tozeur
- Reliable and well-prepared candidates
- Real impact for youth from a remote region
